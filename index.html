Maintenance and Support

Application Maintenance is the critical part of any business. As needs for business change constantly, Application deployed to manage business change as well as additional features are required to be deployed. It is also a challenge for an application outsourcing service provider to work on an existing business critical system. Over the time, we have developed process which understands importance of critical business applications, thus TeksTedia assists you not only in customized application development but also maintening them which ensures smooth operations and reduces the cost of redeveloping and redeploying application.
We provide the following Maintenance and support services.

New features implementation

configuration management

troubleshouting and bug fixing

performance management

change implementation, code review and testing
